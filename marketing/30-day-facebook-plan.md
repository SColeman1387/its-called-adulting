# It's Called Adulting — 30-Day Facebook Campaign
# StoryBrand Voice: Shea Coleman, Dad — Guide, Not Salesman
# Start Date: June 21, 2026

---

## THE VOICE RULES (read before posting anything)
1. **You are never selling.** You are a dad sharing something you made out of fear and love.
2. **Lead with the emotion, not the app.** The app is always the last sentence.
3. **Be specific.** "What a deductible is" beats "financial stuff." Specifics feel real.
4. **Never say "check out."** Say "it's at" or "I put it at."
5. **One link, end of post.** Not in the middle. Not twice.
6. **Short paragraphs.** One or two sentences. Let it breathe.
7. **No exclamation points.** Dads don't sell. Dads talk.
