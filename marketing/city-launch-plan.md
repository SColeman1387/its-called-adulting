## Post Templates by Audience

### Parent Groups
> "My daughter is leaving for college in August and I realized she doesn't know how to do laundry, change a tire, or read a lease. I found this app called It's Called Adulting that walks college-bound kids through 12 life skills — budgeting, cooking, car maintenance, even how to talk to a doctor without mom on the phone. Free to get started. Sharing in case any other parents are panicking like I was. itscalledadulting.com"

### Young Adult Groups  
> "I moved out at 18 and had zero idea how to do anything. Wish something like this existed when I was starting out — It's Called Adulting teaches you the stuff school never did: budgeting, cooking, car maintenance, how to build credit. Worth checking out if you're just getting started. itscalledadulting.com"

### College Prep Groups
> "Senior parents — if your kid doesn't know how to cook, do laundry, or manage a budget yet, there's still time. It's Called Adulting covers all 12 life skills before move-in day. Free to start. itscalledadulting.com"
